Portfolio Sample

July 15th, 2019

By Hai Yu Zhao



One of my favorite design portfolios is from infographic designer, Nicholas Felton.


Founder of Daytum.com and a member of Facebook’s product design team, Felton’s portfolio is laid out in an unconventional way that immediately makes it stand out.


Instead of going for a traditional block layout, Felton’s homepage is just the name of his projects in vibrant colors that match the theme of the project being described.


This simple layout gives his page a clean and minimal feel, and makes it extremely easy to navigate.


For each project page, fletton uses images that capture the highlight’s for each project and he keeps his descriptions concise and to the point.
